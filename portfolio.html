Dream Team allows you to search by pokemon name and number, so fans and new players alike can find their favorite pokemon.

When the user searches a pokemon, Dream Team displays it, along with its typing and stats. The user can search for another pokemon or catch the pokemon, adding it to their team.

After the pokemon is caught, it is added to the six slot team and saved to local storage to be accessed later.
